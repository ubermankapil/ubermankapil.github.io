A Short Autobiography

I am 20 years old. I am tall (about 183 cm) and I weigh about 64 kgs. My eyesight is a little weak mostly because of the long hours I spend in-front of display terminals. I belong to a very diverse Indian joint family of more than 20 people. Like most engineers in India, I usually sleep late at night. I play many sports including both outdoor and indoor. In my primary school and high school, I had achieved many outstanding results. I had cleared all the three levels of National Level of Talent Search Examination, which is the most prestigious and difficult exam for students till grade 10 in India. Also, I have excelled in many Olympiads exams, Holyfaith exam, Macmillian exams.

My failures have motivated me more than my achievements. I work as hard as possible. I am usually good at working under pressure with an upcoming deadline to meet. I also like self-learning usually via web. I hate reading books - novels, technical, academic, etc - all of it. I am not patient enough to read a whole book. I love debating especially technical debate among a small set of people. I am rarely angry. I use to smile always.

I grew up in Akola, Maharashtra along with a number of and friends. Also, I was very popular guy in my primary and my scondary school and so I was having number of friends. So, my childhood was a happy one. I was an excellent student in my primary and high school. I was always good with Mathematics, Mental Ability and Cricket. However, I failed to get a branch of my choice in prestigious IITs and thus I joined Bits Pilani. I made a lot of friends here. I learn a lot here - everyday. I took up a couple of leadership roles too. Currently, I am in my second year. I try to learn from everyone here. I also try to help others as much as possible. I also did an AIESEC Internship at the end of my first year in summer, for which I had travelled to Egypt and had explored almost all of the country.

I use ubermankapil as the username for most of my web accounts. It represents "How super I am!".
